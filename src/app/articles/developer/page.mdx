Embarking on my journey as a developer in the digital realm circa 2010, I first delved into the dynamic landscape of digital marketing. As a Search Engine Optimization Specialist and later an Analyst at a Utah-based marketing agency, I honed strategic thinking, creative flair, and a profound understanding of user behavior. Those early days saw me leading diverse search campaigns anchored in user intent, offering a glimpse into the transformative power of technology in meeting intricate user needs.

Elevating my challenges, I transitioned to more complex problem-solving roles, serving as the Director of E-Commerce at a health foods company and subsequently donning the hats of Chief Brandsmith and Managing Partner at a startup agency. These experiences not only shaped my professional trajectory but also provided heightened exposure to the realm of technology.

A pivotal moment materialized when I undertook a six-month coding bootcamp through the University of Richmond. This immersive experience catapulted me into the expansive domain of full-stack development, equipping me with proficiency in an array of tools and languages, laying the foundation for my journey as a seasoned software engineer.

Three years into the intricate world of software development, my projects span diverse domains, demanding not only technical finesse but also strategic foresight. This narrative encapsulates more than just code; it mirrors my ability to bridge the gap between client expectations and the nuanced intricacies of each project.

As I evolve in this dynamic field, my commitment to continuous learning propels me forward. This story isn't just about technical prowess; it's a testament to navigating diverse landscapes with creativity, adaptability, and an unwavering passion for excellence.
